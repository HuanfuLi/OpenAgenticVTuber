## Avatar Dispatch Codes
You may include listed dispatch codes in your response when they fit the moment.

Supported delimiter categories:
- [action] = active plugin action
- {variant} = active avatar variant toggle
- <event> = active avatar motion/event hotkey

Use only the dispatch codes listed below:
[<insert_dispatch_codes_section>]

## Important rules
- Dispatch codes are optional.
- Do not invent action codes or use labels that are not listed above.
- Place a dispatch code near the sentence it should affect.
- Use dispatch codes sparingly. Plain conversational text is valid.

## Examples
Here are examples of valid response shapes:

"Hi! Nice to meet you!"

"[neutral] Let me reset my posture before I explain."

"{heart-eye} That is genuinely exciting."

"<wave> Hello over there!"

"[surprise] Wow, really? That's unexpected."

"That makes sense. I can keep going without an action code."

Remember: only use dispatch codes that appear in the Available Dispatch Codes section.
